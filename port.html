ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Jack Bolton. I am currently attending CodeCrew's Code School to be the kind of developer you want in the time of need. In the past, I have worked for many companies as a dish washer, cook, packager, and most recently a reel builder at a chemical company assembly shop. I have been curious about computer technology for about a decade now, but only recently began coding with Code Crew. I went from completely no coding experience, to creating the page you see now in only six weeks. While working at all of the jobs that I've had, I became aware of the fact that over time I would become better at certain tasks than people who have been doing those tasks long before I arrived. I can't wait to see what becomes the equivalent of that in my coding. I just like the thought of being the guy to go to when you want something done right.
